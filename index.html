About This Test — Genalyte’s SARS-CoV-2 Serology Panel detects antibodies specific to SARS-CoV-2 antigens (the virus that causes COVID-19). Antibodies are proteins made by your body to attack foreign substances, like viruses or bacteria. The process of developing antibodies is termed an immune response. If you have been exposed to SARS-CoV-2, your body may manufacture antibodies to the virus. This test is designed to detect the overall presence (positive) or absence (negative) of SARS-CoV-2 antibodies.

About This Test — Genalyte has accredited and certified laboratories based in San Diego, California and Austin, Texas. Our tests go through rigorous validation efforts to ensure clinical effectiveness. Unlike other SARS-CoV-2 tests on the market, the Genalyte SARS-CoV-2 serology panel has a very low false-positive rate and has an accuracy rate exceeding 99%.

Disclaimer — Positive and negative calls for overall SARS-CoV-2 antibodies, and individually for IgG and IgM, are generated by a validated machine learning algorithm and may not reflect individual antigen reactivity. This test has not been reviewed by the FDA. Negative results do not rule out SARS-CoV-2 infection, particulary in those who have been in contact with the virus. Follow-up testing with a molecular diagnostic should be considered to rule out infection in these individuals. Results from antibody testing should not be used as the sole basis to diagnose or exclude SARS-CoV-2 infection or to inform infection status. Positive results may be due to past or present infection with non-SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus strains, such as coronavirus HKU1, NL, OC43, or 229E.
